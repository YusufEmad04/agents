About BeautyCare Clinic:
BeautyCare Clinic is a state-of-the-art beauty and wellness center dedicated to enhancing your natural beauty and well-being.
Our team of skilled professionals is committed to providing top-quality services tailored to your unique needs.
We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.


Do you offer gift certificates?
Yes, we offer gift certificates that make perfect gifts for friends and family. You can purchase them at our clinic.

How can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
To cancel or reschedule an appointment, please call our clinic at least 24 hours in advance.

Do you have parking available?
Yes, we have free parking available for our clients.

What safety measures are in place regarding COVID-19?
We have implemented strict sanitation and safety protocols to ensure the health and safety of our clients and staff. This includes regular disinfection, mandatory mask-wearing, and social distancing.
